## Badge

A badge is a tiny label that shows extra info, like a count or status. It's usually used to catch your eye on something important, like unread messages, notifications, or status updates. Think of it as a quick heads-up to grab your attention.

## Intent
Badges roll out in all sorts of colors: primary, secondary, success, info, warning, danger, light, dark, and light/dark. You can even rock your own custom colors.
